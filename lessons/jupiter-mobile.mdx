## Jupiter Mobile: Web3 in the Palm of Your Hand

If you want to get access to onchain tokens and Web3 applications, you need a crypto wallet. You could download a browser extension and navigate through the apps yourself, but this process might not be very beginner-friendly. Luckily for you, nowadays you can get your hands on the entirety of Web3 through your smartphone with Jupiter Mobile!

In recent years, crypto has branched out a lot, with niches like prediction markets, liquidity provision, lending, perps trading, and other applications. Jupiter Mobile allows you to access all of those instruments in a smooth, visually appealing interface. Think of it as a utility superapp for all your crypto needs.

### What Exactly Can You Do on Jupiter Mobile?

First of all, the app serves the function of a [wallet](/lessons/wallet). Upon entering, you can clearly see the value stored in your wallet. All of your SOL and tokens will be displayed here, but if you do not want to show the value of a particular token (it could be a scam with fake value), you can just hide it and it will not add to the total balance.

And when we say the total balance, we **mean** it. Jupiter Mobile will show the value of money stored in DApps, whether it is staked, earning yield, or being utilized as collateral for a perp position. It will also show you all of the claimable rewards that your wallet is eligible for. With Jupiter Mobile, you will never forget where you put your money.

### What If I Don't Have Crypto Yet?

In case you are just getting started in crypto, you can convert fiat to SOL or USDC with the "Add funds" feature. Make your initial deposit with a debit/credit card or just use Apple Pay to get onboarded in less than 5 minutes.

### More Features for Your Convenience

**[Trade](/lessons/jupiter-swap)**

Other crypto wallets will charge you 0.8% for a swap, whereas on Jupiter Mobile it will be only 0.1%. You can choose between market, limit, and recurring orders, as well as trade perps for SOL, ETH, and BTC.

**Magic Link**

If you want to send someone crypto, you can create a "magic link," which is a QR code with an embedded transfer transaction. Simply enter the value you want to send and message it to your friend. Then they can scan the QR and receive the crypto when it is most convenient for them.

**[Jupiter Global](/lessons/jupiter-global)**

One of the most recent additions is Jupiter Global under the "Spend" section. It is a debit card that you can top up with Solana tokens and receive cashbacks in USDC for any purchase. Jupiter Global is available in 200+ countries, with more of them rolling out soon. To receive a card, you need to complete a KYC. You can use Global for QR payments too!

**Reclaim SOL**

After trading tokens, you can reclaim SOL from them. The app will notify you and help with a reclaim process. You can read more about what reclaim is and why it happens in the [Jupiter Reclaim](/lessons/jupiter-reclaim) lesson.

Jupiter Mobile will provide you with the most advanced crypto experience, but be warned: you might stop using your PC for crypto purposes because everything will be available on your phone.
