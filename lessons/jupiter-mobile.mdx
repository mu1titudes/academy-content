## Jupiter Mobile: Solana in the Palm of Your Hand

If you want to get access to onchain tokens and Web3 applications, you need a crypto wallet. You could download a browser extension and navigate through the apps yourself, but this process might not be very beginner-friendly. Luckily for you, nowadays you can get your hands on the entirety of Solana through your smartphone with Jupiter Mobile!

In recent years, crypto has branched out a lot, with niches like prediction markets, liquidity provision, lending, perps trading, and other applications. Jupiter Mobile allows you to access all of those instruments in a smooth, visually appealing interface. Think of it as a utility superapp for all your crypto needs.

### What Exactly Can You Do on Jupiter Mobile?

First of all, the app serves the function of a [wallet](/lessons/wallet). Upon entering, you can clearly see the value stored in your wallet. All of your SOL and tokens will be displayed here, but if you do not want to show the value of a particular token (it could be a scam with fake value), you can just hide it and it will not add to the total balance.

And when we say the total balance, we mean it. Jupiter Mobile will show the value of money stored in dApps, whether it is staked, earning yield, or being utilized as collateral for a perp position. It will also show you all of the claimable rewards that your wallet is eligible for. With Jupiter Mobile, you will never forget where you put your money.

### What If I Don't Have Crypto Yet?

In case you are just getting started in crypto, you can convert fiat to SOL with the "Add Funds" feature. Make your initial deposit with a debit/credit card, or just use Apple or Google Pay to get onboarded in less than 5 minutes.

### Two Ways to Get Started

Jupiter Mobile gives you a choice when you first open the app - between a traditional recovery-phrase [wallet](/lessons/wallet) and a **Quick Account**, the latter being the easiest way to onboard someone new to crypto.

- **Quick Account**: the low-friction path. Powered by Privy under the hood, it lets you create a wallet using a social login (email, Apple, or Google) without ever generating a recovery phrase. You can later export the private key for backup, but only through the Jupiter website (not from inside the app).
- **Recovery-phrase wallet**: the classic self-custodial setup. The app generates 12 or 24 words that act as the [master key](/lessons/safety-private-keys), and you're responsible for writing them down and keeping them safe. Lose the phrase and there's no path to recover the wallet - Jupiter doesn't store it.

### More Features for Your Convenience

**[Trade](/lessons/jupiter-swap)**

Jupiter Mobile gives you Jupiter's full trading stack from inside the app. You can place Instant swaps, limit orders, recurring orders, as well as trade perps for SOL, ETH, and wBTC. Fees on Mobile are competitive: 0% to 0.5% for Instant swaps depending on the pair's volatility, and a flat 0.1% for Limit and Recurring orders.

**Magic Link**

Magic Links let you send tokens to anyone on Solana - even people who don't have a wallet yet. Simply create a "Magic Link" from your Jupiter Mobile, which is a QR code with an embedded transfer transaction. Enter the value you want to send and message it to your friend. They can then tap the link to open Jupiter Mobile (or install it if they haven't yet) and confirm to claim. Unclaimed links expire after 7 days and tokens are automatically returned to your wallet. You can also manually revoke an active link from your Send history.

**[Jupiter Global](/lessons/jupiter-global)**

Jupiter Global is a crypto card that you can top up with USDC and receive cashbacks for purchases. Features include a digital payment card accepted at 150M+ Visa merchants worldwide, Jupiter QR Pay to various APAC merchants with 0% fees, and fiat remittance to over 200 countries. To receive a card, you simply need to complete a KYC.

**dApp Browser**

Your gateway to the broader Solana ecosystem. Tap the globe icon in the top right, search for the dApp you want (Meteora, Sanctum, prediction markets, NFT marketplaces - anything Solana-native), and connect your Jupiter Mobile wallet. From there, you can interact with that dApp as if you were using a desktop wallet extension. This is the feature that makes "superapp" an accurate label - your wallet, Jupiter's core products, and the rest of Solana all run from a single app.

**Pro Tab**

The Pro Tab gives you professional-grade token research tools, such as deep token discovery and tracking, live feeds of trending and newly-launched tokens, launchpad analytics, and a tokenized-stocks section. Includes price charts, market cap, supply data, Verified Insights from [Jupiter VRFD](/lessons/jupiter-vrfd), and an **Organic Score** - a real-time signal of holder count, volume, and liquidity calculated from human wallet activity only.

**Reclaim SOL**

After trading tokens, you can reclaim SOL from them. The app will notify you and help with a reclaim process. You can read more about what reclaim is and why it happens in the [Jupiter Reclaim](/lessons/jupiter-reclaim) lesson.
