## Managing Your Assets with Jupiter Portfolio

**Jupiter Portfolio** is your unified command center on <Term id="solana">Solana</Term>. Instead of manually checking multiple platforms to monitor your assets or rewards, Jupiter Portfolio scans everything in real-time to provide a single, clear view of your net worth. It's designed to help you track performance, manage open positions, and ensure you never leave any assets behind.

### Your Unified Dashboard

The primary goal of the Jupiter Portfolio tool is to remove the guesswork from decentralized finance. When you connect your wallet, the dashboard automatically categorizes your assets so you can see exactly where your capital is allocated.

- **Broad <Term id="protocol">Protocol</Term> Coverage:** Jupiter Portfolio tracks your positions across swaps, lending vaults (including Earn), and even external yield farms
- **Claimables Detection:** To make sure you never forget anything onchain, it scans the network for airdrops, rewards, or idle funds you may have forgotten to collect across the ecosystem and groups them all together
- **Net Worth History:** For a better perspective on your growth, Jupiter Portfolio provides a visual history of your balance changes over time, helping you understand your long-term trends

### PnL and Performance Tracking

Professional asset management requires accurate data. Jupiter Portfolio provides detailed **Profit and Loss (<Term id="pnl">PnL</Term>)** metrics so you can evaluate your strategies and track your progress.

- **Realized vs. Unrealized:** You can easily see the difference between the profits you've already secured and the current value of your active positions
- **Asset-Specific Insights:** By selecting a specific token, you can view your trading history, average entry price, and how that asset is performing relative to your overall holdings

### Privacy and Security

As with all products in the Jupiter ecosystem, safety is a core priority. The Jupiter Portfolio tool is safe by design: it only reads public onchain data and never has access to or control over your funds:

- **Read-Only:** The portfolio tracker reads public data directly from the <Term id="solana">Solana</Term> blockchain to display your balances and positions
- **You Stay in Control:** Your assets always remain in your wallet. Only you can approve transactions
- **Multi-Wallet Support:** Add multiple wallet addresses or domains (like .sol names) to view all your positions across different wallets in one place

## Accessing Jupiter Portfolio

### Step 1: Connect and Scan

Navigate to [jup.ag/portfolio](https://jup.ag/portfolio). Once you connect your <Term id="solana">Solana</Term> wallet, the tool begins a deep scan of the blockchain to identify your assets and active positions.

### Step 2: Reviewing Your Positions

Scroll through the **Assets** tab to view your liquid tokens. Use the **DeFi** tab to see active stakes, loans in Jupiter Lend, or liquidity you've provided to Earn vaults.

### Step 3: Identifying Missed Rewards

Check the **"Claimable"** section regularly. If the tool detects rewards from a recent airdrop or an older liquidity pool, it will give you the exact link to claim those funds.
