## Discover xStocks Onchain with Jupiter

xStocks are available **24/7 on Solana** and can be accessed by anyone. Before buying them or using more advanced strategies, you should verify that the asset you plan to buy is a legitimate token issued by xStocks.

Using the **Jupiter Terminal**, you can track each tokenized stock, confirm that it is an authentic xStocks asset, and follow the price evolution of each token. Jupiter Terminal also provides detailed market data and analytics, allowing you to explore trading activity and liquidity across the <Term id="solana">Solana</Term> ecosystem.

Jupiter Terminal includes a dedicated **"Stocks" tab**, where you can discover and track the tokenized stocks market, including xStocks assets. For each token you can find important information such as:

* <Term id="token">Token</Term> price and price evolution  
* Discounts or premiums compared to the real equity market price  
* Underlying company market capitalization  
* Trading volume  
* Number of holders  
* Available onchain liquidity: essential for trading outside of US market hours

In addition, by clicking on a specific asset, you can access more detailed information and directly **swap to buy or sell the tokenized asset using Jupiter Swap**.

## Buy your first xStocks

After exploring the tokenized stocks market using **Jupiter Terminal**, you can buy xStocks directly from [jup.ag](https://jup.ag/) or from the Terminal interface.

Steps to buy xStocks:

* Connect your <Term id="solana">Solana</Term> wallet to [jup.ag](http://jup.ag)  
* Select the xStocks asset you want to buy  
* <Term id="swap">Swap</Term> from **SOL, USDC, or another supported token**  
<small>*To have the best experience, always swap from USDC to the xStocks asset. This routes directly through xStocks xChange system that draws from their offchain liquidity.*</small>
* Receive the token directly in your wallet

On Jupiter <Term id="swap">Swap</Term> you can also use two additional order types to buy xStocks.

**Limit Order:** Set the price at which you want to buy an xStocks token. Your order will automatically be executed when the asset reaches the target price.

**Recurring Order:** This feature allows you to invest the same amount on a regular basis. For example, if you want to buy $50 of TSLAx every month, you can automate this strategy using Jupiter's Recurring order, making it easier to build long-term positions through dollar-cost averaging (DCA).

Before confirming any transaction, always verify that the token is **"Verified"** and clearly labeled as an **xStocks asset**, in order to avoid potential scams or fake tokens.
