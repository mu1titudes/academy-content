## Tokenized equities, the new Capital Market

Equities represent shares of companies and offer different benefits such as voting power, dividends, and exposure to a company's performance.

These assets are a key component of the traditional financial system and attract investors across the world because of their characteristics and long-term value creation.

As blockchain technology has grown, a new type of capital market has started to emerge. Investment in equities can now evolve in a different way thanks to blockchain infrastructure.

In this lesson, you will discover how tokenized equities work and how they are transforming the way people access global financial markets.

---

## What's tokenized equities?

<Term id="blockchain">Blockchain</Term> offers a new way for financial markets to evolve, move, and follow capital flows. With infrastructure available **24/7**, a new paradigm has emerged, starting with crypto-native assets like Bitcoin, Ethereum, or Solana, and expanding to new classes of digital and tokenized financial products.

Tokenized equities are a class of digital assets that **track the price of a real-world stock held by an issuer**.

The issuer is a private company that acquires and holds shares of the underlying asset and then issues blockchain tokens representing exposure to that asset.

These tokens allow users to gain exposure to the performance of traditional stocks **directly onchain**, using a [wallet](/lessons/wallet) instead of a traditional brokerage account.

## Why tokenized equities are powerful

The equity market is one of the most attractive & powerful markets in the world, but is still inefficient in some aspects. That's what tokenized equities can address thanks to public blockchain composability.

This new class of assets brings several advantages compared to traditional equities.

### Global access

* Thanks to the blockchain, tokenized equities can be accessed globally using a wallet such as [**Jupiter Mobile**](https://jup.ag/mobile), allowing users to hold assets in **self-custody onchain**.  
* Traditional stocks must be held through brokerage accounts or banks, which often involve geographic restrictions, account creation, and regulatory limitations.  
* Tokenized equities remove many of these barriers by making asset access **more open and global**.

---

### Peer-to-peer and fractionability

* Accessibility is one of the most powerful aspects of tokenized equities. In traditional markets, stocks are typically purchased through intermediaries and held within brokerage accounts.  
* Tokenized equities change this dynamic.  
* Because these equities are tokenized, they can be **fractionally owned**, meaning users can buy a small portion of an asset instead of an entire share. This allows anyone to start investing with small amounts of capital.  
* And because they exist on blockchain networks, these assets can also be **sent via peer-to-peer** between wallets anywhere in the world, with transaction fees limited to the blockchain network cost, which is extremely low on <Term id="solana">**Solana**</Term>. This is practically impossible for traditional brokerages.

---

### Open markets

* Traditional stock markets operate during limited hours, only five days per week, and close on public holidays.  
* Tokenized equities, however, can be traded **24/7 on blockchain infrastructure**, allowing continuous market access and faster reactions to global events.  
* This provides investors with more flexibility and constant exposure to market opportunities.

---

### Composability

* Traditional equities are typically locked inside brokerage accounts and cannot easily be used for additional financial strategies.  
* Tokenized equities unlock **DeFi composability**.  
* Because these assets exist onchain, they can interact with <Term id="defi">DeFi</Term> protocols. Holders can remain exposed to the performance of the underlying asset while simultaneously putting their assets to work, for example by generating yield or borrowing liquidity through <Term id="defi">DeFi</Term> platforms such as [**Jupiter Lend**](/lessons/jupiter-borrow).

---

The equity market is one of the largest and most attractive financial markets in the world, but parts of its infrastructure remain inefficient.

Tokenized equities help solve some of these limitations by leveraging the transparency, accessibility, and composability of public blockchains.
