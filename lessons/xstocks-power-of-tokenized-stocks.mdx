Buying tokenized stocks is the first step offering access to equities to anyone, anywhere, with minimal capital requirements thanks to the **fractionability of tokens**.

But the real value of tokenized assets lies in their **transferability and composability** within the <Term id="defi">DeFi</Term> ecosystem.

## From holding to using

<Term id="defi">DeFi</Term> offers many ways to make tokenized assets productive and increase the value of keeping them onchain.

The most powerful use case is <Term id="lending-pool">**lending**</Term> and <Term id="borrow-rate">**borrowing**</Term>, unlocking a new paradigm for everyday equity holders.

xStocks assets are available on **JupLend**, offering new use cases to everyone:

* Earn yield on top of xStocks by providing them as collateral when lending is incentivized  
* Borrow stablecoins using your assets as collateral to fully benefit from their value and increase capital efficiency  
* <Term id="leverage">Leverage</Term> your exposure to xStocks by borrowing liquidity and buying the xStocks asset again (e.g. leverage looping)

Using lending protocols requires understanding the concepts of **lending and borrowing**. By using assets as collateral to borrow liquidity, users can be exposed to **liquidation risk** if the value of their collateral drops below a defined liquidation threshold. This threshold varies depending on the asset and the collateral ratio used.

## Example strategy

A user has **$5,000 to invest in tokenized equities**. Across several assets, they buy:

* $2,000 worth of **SPYx (S\&P 500\)**  
* $2,000 worth of **TSLAx (Tesla)**  
* $1,000 worth of **NVDAx (Nvidia)**

After buying, the user deposits these assets into **Jupiter Lend** to borrow **$1,000 USDC**, while keeping a safe margin to avoid potential liquidation risk if asset prices drop.

The user can now use this liquidity in different ways depending on their strategy:

* Increase their xStocks exposure by buying more assets (leverage looping, optimized if asset prices increase)  
* Generate yield using strategies offering returns greater than the borrowing cost  
* Use this liquidity for other <Term id="defi">DeFi</Term> opportunities
