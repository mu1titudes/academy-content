## Keeping Your Wallet Clean with Jupiter Reclaim

The first time you acquire a unique token on Solana, you need to pay a small deposit or rent price of approximately 0.002 SOL to create a dedicated token account. The token account will then store your token balance info on the blockchain. The rent price is refundable - that's why you can reclaim it once you are done interacting with the token.

Many users - both crypto newbies and natives - often forget about this rent, and over time this rent can accumulate to a fairly large amount. The simple act of reclaiming your rent is like finding old money in your pants!

### Why is Rent Required?

Solana is a transparent blockchain - every single bit of information has to be stored and cross-referenced. Rent compensates validators for storing your account's data onchain. Without it, anyone could create accounts indefinitely with no cost, bloating the network state.

Once you sell off all of one particular token, that token account is now empty - but the rent is not automatically reclaimed. This means that a high-activity wallet might have a decent amount of SOL sitting around as rent, unclaimed!

### Rent Scales with Transactions

While the rent price may seem small for now, it can scale very quickly with active trading. The rent price is deducted every time you trade tokens on Solana. If you trade 500 different memecoins, you will open 500 token accounts and the value of the rent reclaim will be roughly 1 SOL (about 0.002 SOL per account).

Nevertheless, to initiate reclaim, you need to make sure that the token balance is equal to 0. So before proceeding with refunding rent, sell the rest of your tokens with Jupiter Swap.

### How to Reclaim Token Account Rent

**Step 1: Navigate to Jupiter Portfolio**

Navigate to [Jupiter Portfolio](https://jup.ag/portfolio). If there are any potential deposits that are eligible to be reclaimed, you'll find the "Reclaim" button is active.

![Reclaim Button](https://static.academy.jup.ag/images/illustrations/reclaim_button.avif)

**Step 2: Click on the "Reclaim" Button**

Click on the "Reclaim" button. Select the tokens you have finished trading and reclaim your SOL from them.

![Reclaim Prompt](https://static.academy.jup.ag/images/illustrations/reclaim_prompt.avif)

Note: Rent Reclaim is not compatible with Trust Wallet's in-app browser.
