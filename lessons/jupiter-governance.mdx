## Shaping the Future with Jupiter Governance

In traditional finance, shareholders of a public company have the right to vote on major decisions at an Annual General Meeting. But voting participation requires going through brokers or other third-parties, and practically speaking, most retail shareholders never vote at all.

Jupiter <Term id="dao">DAO</Term> <Term id="governance">Governance</Term> removes every layer of that friction. Launched in 2024, it gave the community a direct right to express itself and shape the future of Jupiter - big issues like treasury allocations & tokenomics changes. No intermediaries or brokers, just your wallet and your stake.

Not only does Jupiter ship products, they shipped a whole new paradigm around decentralized financial governance.

### What is a DAO?

<Term id="dao">DAO</Term> stands for Decentralized Autonomous Organization. Rather than being governed by a board of directors or a management team operating behind closed doors, a <Term id="dao">DAO</Term> is governed by its community through smart contracts on the blockchain. Its features are literally derived from its name:

* **Decentralized:** By utilizing the blockchain to gather votes, it provides global access to anyone with the correct stake in Jupiter to participate. This swiftly eliminates the need for any intermediaries or other barriers.
* **Autonomous:** All votes are automatically run and tallied via smart contracts, with every vote recorded on-chain and transparent to anyone.

### The Jupiter DAO

The Jupiter <Term id="dao">DAO</Term> is the governance layer of the entire Jupiter ecosystem. The aim is clear: to level up passive token holders into active participants with a real stake in the protocol's direction, allow them to participate in decision-making, and shift Jupiter towards a global collaboration.

Past votes have included:
- A 30% reduction in the total supply of the <Term id="jup">JUP</Term> token
- A strategic partnership with Huma Finance
- The allocation of the DAO's operating budget

These are not opinion polls from the community, but rather, binding on-chain decisions that directly affect how Jupiter operates.

### Voting Power: Staking Your JUP

To gain voting power, you will need to obtain and stake your <Term id="jup">JUP</Term> tokens.

Your voting power is proportional to your share of staked tokens in the entire <Term id="dao">DAO</Term> pool. The higher the percentage of your <Term id="jup">JUP</Term> tokens in the stake pool, the more your voice counts.

* **Non-Custodial:** Your tokens remain yours. Staked <Term id="jup">JUP</Term> is locked in a smart contract that you control and not held by a third party.
* **Unstaking & Cool-Down Period:** You can unstake at any time, but a 7-day cool-down period applies before your tokens are returned to your wallet. Crucially, your voting power does not decrease during this window, which will allow you to continue participating in active proposals until the cool-down completes.

### Active Staking Rewards

While you help Jupiter shape the future of on-chain finance, there's the added bonus of getting rewarded via the Active <Term id="staking">Staking</Term> Rewards (ASR)!

Created alongside the Jupiter DAO, the ASR is a quarterly incentive program that distributes <Term id="jup">JUP</Term> tokens to stakers who actively participate in <Term id="dao">DAO</Term> votes. The Jupiter team allocates a total yearly budget of 200 million <Term id="jup">JUP</Term> tokens, and distributes 50 million tokens every quarter.

* **Eligibility:** Rewards are calculated based on your voting power relative to the total staked supply at the time of the vote. <Term id="staking">Staking</Term> alone is not enough - you must actively vote to qualify.
* **Claiming:** At the end of each quarter, your rewards become claimable from the <Term id="dao">DAO</Term> dashboard. You have until the end of the following quarter to claim them.
* **Unclaimed Rewards:** If you do not claim before that deadline, the unclaimed <Term id="jup">JUP</Term> is returned to the community treasury to fund the next ASR cycle.

Your estimated annualized return is visible directly on the staking page, so you can evaluate the yield before committing.

### JUP Research

As you might expect, Jupiter <Term id="governance">Governance</Term> is one of the biggest DAOs on <Term id="solana">Solana</Term>. Each and every vote is impactful and causes ripples of excitement within the community. To help gather all the conversations into a dedicated space, Jupiter created the <Term id="jup">JUP</Term> Research platform.

There, the community and dev team can propose early drafts of votes, collect preliminary feedback, and adjust the plan accordingly. It's a space to let members of the <Term id="dao">DAO</Term> express themselves early, or even provide fresh ideas for the next vote on Jupiter's plan.

### How Do You Join?

**Step 1: Navigate to the <Term id="governance">Governance</Term> Portal**

Go to [vote.jup.ag](https://vote.jup.ag) and connect your <Term id="solana">Solana</Term> wallet.

**Step 2: Stake Your JUP**

Click "Stake JUP" on the main page. Enter the amount you wish to stake and approve the transaction in your wallet. Your voting power is now active.

**Step 3: Browse and Vote on Active Proposals**

From the governance dashboard, you can see all currently active votes. Review each proposal, read the associated <Term id="jup">JUP</Term> Research discussion if available, and cast your vote on-chain.

**Step 4: Claim Your ASR Rewards**

At the end of each quarter, return to the dashboard to claim your ASR allocation. Remember: unclaimed rewards expire at the end of the following quarter.

Congrats! You are now a certified <Term id="jup">JUP</Term> staker and are ready to participate in the governance of on-chain finance!
